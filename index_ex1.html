Night Safari visitors

Problem statement

in 2020, the worlds first nocturnal zoo, the Night Safariin singapore, attracted approximately 343 thousand
visitors. The Night Safari is part of the four wildlife-centred liesure attractions in singapore, which includes the:

  • Singapore Zoo
  • Jurong Bird Park
  • River Safari

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Night Safari was closed from April to June 2020, and has since
then operated at limited capacity under safety measures.

The following table shows the number of visitors to Night Safari from 2011 to 2020.
